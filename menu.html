Our Menu

Breakfast Classics

Start your day right with our breakfast classics. From the hearty Full Irish Breakfast to the flavorful Steak and Eggs, we've got your morning cravings covered. You can also create your own breakfast masterpiece. For something a bit more indulgent, try our Fluffy Pancakes. Our omelets come in various flavors, including Ham and Cheese, Florentine, and Ranchero. If you're looking for a healthy option, don't miss our Overnight Oats, Greek Yogurt Smoothie, or Breakfast Crunch Salad.

Delectable Pastries

Pair your morning coffee with our selection of pastries. From the classic Plain Croissant to the savory Ham and Cheese Croissant, we offer a variety of options to satisfy your pastry cravings. Try our Almond Croissant for a sweet treat, or enjoy our freshly baked Scones served with clotted cream and jam.

Sides for Every Meal

Whether you're enjoying a main dish or just looking for a tasty snack, our sides are the perfect complement to your meal. Choose from Chips, Garlic Fries, Parmesan Fries, Side Salad, or Sweet Potato Fries to complete your dining experience.

Kids' Favorites

We haven't forgotten the little ones. Our Kids' Menu includes Goujons Chips, Tomato Pasta, and Pancakes, ensuring that even the pickiest eaters find something they love.

Nutrient-Packed Salads

For those seeking a lighter option, our salad selection includes a Superfood Salad with a nutritious blend of superfoods, a Goat Cheese Salad with candied walnuts and creamy goat cheese, and a classic Caesar Salad with crisp romaine, croutons, and Caesar dressing.

Mouthwatering Mains

Our mains include the rich and flavorful Butter Chicken Gravy and a Grilled Salmon Steak cooked to perfection. Each dish is prepared with care to ensure a satisfying dining experience.

Pasta Perfection

Indulge in our pasta dishes, including Spaghetti Carbonara, Fettuccine Alfredo, Penne Arrabiata, and Tortellini Al Pesto. Each pasta creation is a culinary masterpiece.

Irresistible Burgers

Our burger selection features classics like the Classic Cheese, Pulled Beef Brisket, and Mexican Chicken, along with a hearty Vegetarian option. These burgers are crafted to perfection, offering a burst of flavors with every bite.
