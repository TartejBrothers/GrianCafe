About Us

Looking at the face pace demand of corporate structures in Ireland, Skipero Limited (referred as the Company or Employer hereinafter) was established in the year 2023 to meet the catering demand in corporate houses and corporate events. The Company received a warm welcome from corporate houses in Ireland for its overwhelming catering services. The company is providing its products and services to multinational giant business entities. Event management and catering is the core activity of the business.
